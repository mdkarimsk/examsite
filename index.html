    What is J.C. Bose MTSE ?
  • Jagadish Chandra Bose Mathamatics Talent Search Examination(J.C. bose mtse) is a mathematics-based competition conducted each year within the last week of december to last week of january by the PALLI DIPAWAN CLUB(PDC).
  • The Examination conducted for students of Class 5 to 10 aims to identify and support students who show a potential of becoming future scientists or technologists.
  • Each student is provided a Student Performance Report (SPR). The SPR analyses the strong and weak areas of the student and also gives a detailed performance comparison of the student with other students at School, area,and distric level.
  • There are two levels in this examination,level-1 and level-2 or final level.
    • Eligibility criteria
  • Students of classes 1 to 10 are eligible to appear for the 1st level test.There is no other eligibility criterion like minimum marks.
    • Selection procedure
  • Each student who will get a minimum 50% of total marks in level-1 test,eligible for the next level means for the final level or level-2
  • Top 5% of total students appear for the final level will get economical help and a medal
